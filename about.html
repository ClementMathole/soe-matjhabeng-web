Quick Questions

Get your quick questions answered here

What is the purpose of this project?

This project aims to research, document, and showcase the different dance styles in the Matjhabeng community, highlighting their history, cultural significance, and artistic elements.

Which dances are featured in this project?

The project focuses on at least 4 dance styles, including traditional, contemporary, and unique dances that are significant to the community.

How was the research conducted?

The team conducted research through historical studies, community interviews, and engagement with locals.

Why is the project important?

The project preserves and promote local dance traditions.

About Us

We are a team of students dedicated to showcase the beauty, energy, and heritage of Matjhabeng's dance traditions. Our mission is to uplift and document dance styles in Matjhabeng through visuals, storytelling, and research. This website is part of our project, where we chose to explore how dance connects people, cultures, and social backgrounds in Matjhabeng.
