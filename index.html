Welcome to our Digital
exploration of the vibrant dances
of the Matjhabeng Community.

This project aims to document and celebrate the diverse dance
forms that contribute to the rich culture and heritage of
Matjhabeng.

Through research and engagement, we have uncovered the
historical significance, cultural importance, and artistic beauty of
dances.

Join us as we delve into the unique stories told through movement.

Project Timeline

Dance Perfomances in Matjhabeng

1 - Day

Project Initiation

A team was formed, assigned roles, and outlined project objectives. After brainstorming, we selected 4 dance styles and created a structured plan with deadlines.

3 - Weeks ( March 03 - 14 )

Research Phase

We conducted a research on each dance's history, cultural significance, and artistic elements. We also gathered images and references.

16 - Days ( March 03 - April 05 )

Content Development

Our research findings were structured into engaging narratives. We documented each dance's history, music, costumes, and role in the community.

3 - Days ( March 17 - 19 )

Engaging With The Community

Community engagement initiated, to get more accurate information.

4 - Weeks ( March 24 - April 04 )

Website Development & Design

We started building the website, designing every page. The layout was made visually appealing and user-friendly.

2 - Days ( April - 08 )

Multimedia Intergration & Refinement

Photos and videos were uploaded to enhane content. We ensured proper formatting for smooth playback and optimized media files for better perfomance.

4 - Days ( April 04 - 09 )

Ensuring Proper Permissions

All necessary permissions were verified and secured to allow the integration of media findings and resources.

4 - Days ( April 04 - 09 )

Presentation Preperation

We created a powerPoint summarizing our research and website features.

3 - Days ( April 07 - 09 )

Final Review & Testing

We proofred content, tested navigation and responsiveness, and fixed any issues.

2 - Days ( April 08 - 09 )

Website Publication

We published the website, making our information accessible online.

1 - Day ( April 09 )

Final Submission

Final adjustments were made before submission.
